FAQ

Good to know

Is my photo uploaded anywhere?

No. The AI model runs entirely in your browser using your device's own processing power. Your photo never leaves your computer.

Does it work on every photo?

It works best on photos with a person clearly in frame. Busy backgrounds or unusual poses can trip up the AI — that's what the manual touch-up brush is for.

Do I need to sign up?

No account needed. Just open the page and upload a photo.

Is it really free?

Yes — Cutout is currently free to use while it's in early beta.
